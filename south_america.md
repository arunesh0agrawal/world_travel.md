I think **Phase 5** is where the philosophy becomes simplest: **South America is so large that most countries deserve their own trip**. The only countries I would combine are those where the overland route is genuinely easy and the combined itinerary still feels relaxed.

## ✅ Why this version?

Compared to earlier drafts, this version **doesn't force combinations just because countries are neighbors**. Large countries remain standalone, while only two pairings—**Guatemala + Belize** and **Costa Rica + Panama**—are kept because they are compact, well connected, and comfortably explored within your target of **7–14 days**. Everything else is given enough time to be enjoyed without feeling rushed.
