I think we can make **Phase 2 (Asia)** much better than the previous version by following the same philosophy we used for Europe:

* ✅ Ideal trip = **7–14 days**
* ✅ Combine only if it feels natural
* ✅ Don't force countries together
* 🔴 = Country deserves **more than 14 days**
* 🚆 = Train
* 🚗 = Road
* ✈️ = Flight
* 🚢 = Ferry

## One adjustment I'd consider

The only grouping I'd keep as a multi-country itinerary is the **Silk Road** (Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan), because those countries genuinely complement each other as a single journey. For the rest of Asia, most destinations are strong enough to stand on their own rather than being combined just because they're nearby. This keeps each trip enjoyable and avoids feeling rushed.
