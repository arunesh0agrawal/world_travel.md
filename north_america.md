I think **North America should stay simple**. Unlike Europe, there are only three sovereign countries in mainland North America (Canada, the United States, and Mexico), but each is large enough that you shouldn't try to "cover it all" in one trip.

Following our rules:

* ✅ Ideal trip = **7–14 days**
* ✅ Only combine if it genuinely makes sense
* 🔴 = More than 14 days recommended
* 🚗 = Best by road
* 🚆 = Best by train
* ✈️ = Flights recommended

---

# 🌎 Phase 4 – North America (Optimized)

> **Travel Philosophy**
>
> Don't try to combine Canada, the USA, and Mexico into one vacation. Each offers a distinct experience and deserves dedicated time.

---
