Excellent. I think **Africa should be planned very differently from Europe or Asia.**

Unlike Europe, most people don't travel between several African countries by train. Trips are usually centered around:

* 🦁 Safari circuits
* 🏜 Desert adventures
* 🏖 Islands
* 🏛 Ancient civilizations
* 🚗 Self-drive routes

So the grouping philosophy changes slightly.

## 🔥 One change I'd make to the overall project

After finishing Africa, I would actually split the remaining world into:

* 🌎 **Phase 4 – North America**
* 🌎 **Phase 5 – Central & South America**
* 🌴 **Phase 6 – Caribbean**
* 🌊 **Phase 7 – Oceania & Pacific Islands**
* 🕌 **Phase 8 – Middle East & Caucasus**

This structure keeps each phase focused and avoids mixing regions with very different travel styles. It's also much easier to use as a long-term travel planner.
