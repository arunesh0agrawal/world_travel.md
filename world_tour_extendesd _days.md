# ⭐ My Personal Lifetime Plan

If I were planning this for myself, I wouldn't force every vacation into a two-week window. I'd categorize them like this:

🟢 **1 week**

* Small countries and islands (e.g., Luxembourg, Malta, Bhutan, Maldives, Fiji)

🟡 **2 weeks**

* Most regional combinations (e.g., Central Europe, Kenya + Tanzania, Thailand + Cambodia + Vietnam)

🟠 **3 weeks**

* Countries or combinations with a lot to see (e.g., Japan, France, Spain + Portugal, Türkiye, South Africa)

🔴 **4 weeks or more**

* Vast countries where travel distances are part of the experience (e.g., China, Brazil, Australia, the United States if you're exploring multiple regions)

This approach gives you enough time to actually experience each destination instead of spending a large portion of your trip in transit. Over a lifetime, it's a much more satisfying way to see the world.
