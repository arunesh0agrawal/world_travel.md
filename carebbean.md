Perfect. **Phase 6 will cover the Caribbean**, because these islands are very different from mainland North or South America.

This is probably the hardest region to optimize because **distance on the map is misleading**. Some islands are close geographically but have poor flight connections.

So our rules remain:

* ✅ Ideal trip: **5–14 days**
* ✅ Combine only if flights/ferries make sense.
* ✅ Don't combine just because islands are nearby.
* ✈️ = Flight
* ⛴ = Ferry
* 🏖 = Beach Holiday

## ✅ Why this version?

Unlike earlier drafts, I **don't force island-hopping** just because islands appear close on a map. Most Caribbean destinations are enjoyable as **their own 5–10 day holiday**. The only exception is the **Lesser Antilles cruise**, where the cruise itself becomes the transport, making multiple islands practical without feeling rushed. This keeps the plan aligned with your philosophy of relaxed, memorable trips rather than maximizing the number of countries visited.
